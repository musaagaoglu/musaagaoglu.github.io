Working paper
Signaling in Informal Contracts — A Field Experiment in Volunteer Management
Single author · draft in preparation, 2025
A field experiment (n = 683) at a large-scale event evaluating signaling mechanisms in volunteer management. Formal commitment significantly reduces no-shows and late cancellations but decreases overall shift completion; trust-based signals do not affect no-show rates but improve organizational efficiency by fostering earlier cancellations — highlighting the trade-offs between mandatory confirmation and relational signaling.
